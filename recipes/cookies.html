Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Cookies are by far one of my most favorite sweet treats. It can be enjoyed with a glass of milk or a bowl of your favoritec icecream. With or without, this is an easy recipe that will indulge you in chocolate heaven.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 2 tsp. hot water
  • 1/2 tsp. salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a large bown, mix together butter and sugar until smooth. Add in eggs and stir in the vanilla.
  3. In a seperate bowl, dissolve the baking soda in hot water.
  4. Add to the batter and mix in salt.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips, flour, and nuts (optional).
  6. Place spoonfuls of batter onto a lined baking sheet (roll into small balls for a more consistent shape).
  7. Bake for aprox. 8-10 minutes in the oven or until golden brown.
  8. Prepare a bowl of your favorite icecream.
  9. Enjoy!
