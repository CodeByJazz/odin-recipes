Avocado Toast

Description

What's both creamy and crip- this delicious avacoda toast! If you're looking for an easy breakfast recipe look no further. This can be a delicious snack or meal fit for every diet.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 fresh avocado
  • sea salt
  • ground pepper
  • Any addtional toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Toast bread in a conventional or toaster oven.
  2. Carefully remove the pit from the avocado.
  3. Use a spoon to scoop out the flesh. Add to a bowl and mash it with a fork until smooth.
  4. Mix in salt and pepper to taste (about 1/8 teaspoon).
  5. Spread the mixture onto your toast. Add any extra toppings of your choosing.
  6. Enjoy!
