Creamy Baked Mac and Cheese

Description

The best time of the year is when I get to have a bite of my mother's homestyle cooking. This mac and cheese is a family recipe from South Carolina that is bound to have you making it more than just once. It's oven-baked giving you the most flavor from every individual ingredient.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. elbow noodles
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 2 1/2 cups evaporated milk
  • 4 cups medium sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups extra sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 tbsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. paprika
  • chives optional

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Fill a large pot with water and bring to a boil.
  3. Add in elbow noodles. Cook for about 3 minutes or until. al dente.
  4. Carefully use a strainer to drain the water from the noodles.
  5. Place the noodles into a 9x12 inch nonstick pan. Add butter to the bottom and sides of the pan to ensure an easy cleanup.
  6. Add salt, pepper, and paprika to taste.
  7. Pour in the remaining butter and mix in both eggs.
  8. Add milk, cheese, and chives to the noodles. Stir until everything is well mixed.
  9. Place the dish into the oven on the top rack. Bake for aprox. 20-25 minutes, until cheese is bubbly and light golden brown.
  10. Remove the dish from the oven and allow it to cool for a few minutes before serving.
  11. Enjoy!
